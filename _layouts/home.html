The Swiss Film Academy of New York is a nonprofit film school located in Manhattan, New York, founded in 2017 by Educasuisse located in Geneva, Switzerland.

The School offers noncredit courses in the arts of filmmaking, producing, directing and screenwriting.
Swiss Film Academy draws faculty who are active, working professionals in their fields, many of whom are award winners or have formerly taught at such prestigious institutions as Tisch School of the Arts and New York Film Academy.
